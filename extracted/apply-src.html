Rules
1. When you send in a staff application or ban appeal it does not mean you will get in or even get a response. The Instellar team looks at all submissions. Decides what to do with them. We can say no to any application. Appeal for any reason or for no reason at all. 2. Getting accepted is not something you can expect to happen. The team chooses who to accept. It is a privilege. If we say no that is it. You cannot appeal our decision. Try to negotiate with us. We can stop, open applications and appeals whenever we want. Just because you meet all the requirements does not mean you will get in. 3. What you write in your application or appeal has to follow the server rules. If you are mean or lie or try to spam us we might say no away. If you are trying to appeal a ban and you do something your ban might get longer or become permanent. Do not. Send in fake appeals. This can get you in trouble. 4. You can only send in one application or appeal, per day. Sending in more does not help you. The staff team looks at what you send in to make sure you are following the rules. We can change these rules whenever we want and the new rules will apply when we look at your application or appeal. If something is not covered in these rules the Owner of Instellar gets to decide what to do. Even if something is covered in these rules the Owner still gets to make the decision.
